Coverage

Improving Test Effectiveness

Measuring and maintaining a high test coverage % help to reduce issues from production. But remember to incorporate user behaviors as early as you can. If your product is not released yet, there are ways to simulate your potential users for example run some dogfooding or beta testing. Learning and analyzing your users at the very early stage gives you valuable data points to create or revise your users flows. This information will be valuable when it comes to prioritizing your test plans. This would give you strong arguments to prioritize test plan execution. This is extremely important in an Agile environment where time and QA resources are very limited.
